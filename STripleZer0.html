From TripleZer0, the man himself.

I TripleZer0, will tell you a true story. As best as i can remmember of how I came to be TripleZer0. Also a few intresting things in my Deep Web history.

Some Ordinary Gamers

It all started back in late 2016, I started wathching SomeOrdinaryGamer's Deep Web Browsing videos. I became addicted and wanted to view this deep web myself.

I got in and started looking arround for some interesting sites. One that stuck out to me was Dans Chat. I became an active user in this chat as username: noone, as in no one. I wanted to stay anonymous like everyone else. I then messed arround a bit with my username to become none000.

Barely a week or two into being there i found out a lot about the deep web. I also kept being called 000 or three zeros, as a nickname. So it needed to be tweaked again. I had decided on TripleZer0. After that username change i was invited to a new chat room of sorts.

EvilCorp

Where evil lurks.

I was starting to enjoy evilcorps friendly users more than dans users. So within a month of being invited i made the change completely to Evilcorp. If you are new here EvilCorp is an irc chat. I made new friends and that is when i began coding as well. I became addicted to chat room. It was my life.
